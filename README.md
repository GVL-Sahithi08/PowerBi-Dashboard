<h2>ğŸ“ˆ Key Insights</h2>
<ul style="font-size:16px;">
  <li>Majority of complaints are related to road damage and drainage issues.</li>
  <li>Most complaints are concentrated in specific areas like Gandhi Nagar.</li>
  <li>Complaint resolution rates vary significantly between departments.</li>
  <li>Total Complaints:50 complaints were recorded across all departments.</li>
  <li>Resolution Rate:15 complaints were resolved, giving a 30% resolution rate.</li>
  <li>Department-wise Trends:Sanitation received the highest number of resolved complaints (6 resolved).</li>
  <li>Parks & Trees had a high number of submitted cases (5 submitted) but fewer resolved ones (1 resolved).</li>
  <li>Electricity, Roads, and Water Supply had almost similar complaint distribution patterns.</li>
  <li>Status Breakdown:Submitted: 42% of complaints (majority are still open).</li>

<li>Resolved: 30% of complaints.
Escalated: 28% of complaints, indicating a need for better follow-up processes.</li>

<li>Time Trend:
The complaints show clusters on specific dates rather than a uniform distribution, suggesting certain events or issues triggered spikes.</li>
</ul>
